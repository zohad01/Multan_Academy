# Online Teaching Platform Proposal

## 1. Project Overview
- **Objective:** Build an online teaching platform for students and teachers.  
- **Reference Website:** Nearpeer.org  
- **Focus:** Video-based courses with support for quizzes, assignments, certificates, and progress tracking.  
- **Target Users:** Students, Teachers, Admins  

---

## 2. Core Features

### **Student Features**
- Registration & login (email-based)  
- Dashboard showing purchased courses, progress, and grades  
- Access to quizzes, assignments, and certificates  
- Secure password management  

### **Teacher Features**
- Registration, login, and profile management  
- Course creation, update, and deletion  
- Support for multiple teachers per subject  

### **Course Management**
- Support for video, PDF, and notes  
- Optional live class integration (Zoom or similar)  
- Progress tracking bar  

### **Payment & Access Control**
- Payment gateway for course enrollment  
- Access only to purchased courses  

### **Admin Panel**
- Manage users (teachers & students)  
- Manage courses, subjects, and categories  
- Admin activity logs  

---

## 3. Security & Compliance
- User data protection  
- PCI-compliant payment safety  
- Video piracy protection (watermarking/encryption)  
- Encrypted passwords  
- Admin activity logging for accountability  

---

## 4. Student Engagement & Performance
- Quizzes & assignments  
- Certificates on course completion  
- Student gradebook  
- Progress tracking  
- Optional future gamification features  

---

## 5. Technical Considerations / Clarifications Needed
- **Video Hosting:** Vimeo, AWS, or own server?  
- **Storage & Cost:** Estimate storage needs & hosting cost  
- **Video Optimization:** Speed, buffering, adaptive streaming  
- **Course Content Types:** Video-only or include PDFs, notes, live classes?  
- **Preferred Stack:** React JS + Node JS + MongoDB (finalize one stack)  
- **Design System & Screens:** Number of screens, dark/light theme, brand colors, design guidelines  
- **Support & Maintenance:** Bug fixing, updates, 6-month technical support  

---

## 6. Recommendations
- Finalize one technology stack to reduce complexity  
- Confirm course content types & video hosting method before development  
- Keep mobile app or future expansion optional initially  
- Ensure responsive, SEO-friendly design for better ranking
